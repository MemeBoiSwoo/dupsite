Fashion Buying Office

Zero Traders Inc. was established in 1993 as an International Fashion, Marketing, Consulting and Buying Office. We provide a unique personalized service to retailers and distributors worldwide. We offer a full range of services, within the context of fashion and fashion related industries including home, and accessories.

Apparel OEM Manufacturing in Japan and US

Not much knowledge about apparel production is required to get started, because we provide full support with the technical side of the clothing manufacturing process. All we need to get started with is some original samples and examples of fabrics or design file. From there, our clothing production experts will guide through the necessary steps to make custom clothing. After the initial design, samples are created reflecting the perfect garment production process for your idea. It is our particular strength to be your small quantity clothing manufacturer. Our garment manufacturing process seamlessly integrates your idea into a professional production line at the best garment factories and suppliers.

*Manufacturing in Japan – Low minimums and fast leading time with the best quality control. Highest quality in the world, and high–end Japanese fabrics are available. We provide from fabrics to pattern making to samples to finished production, like a one–stop shop.

Types of garments we can make:

  • Jeans
  • Jackets
  • Coats
  • Sweaters/Knits
  • Shirts
  • Trousers
  • T–Shirts
  • Jerseys

*Manufacturing in US – Custom t–shirt printing and embroidery in New York City. Cut and sewn in Los Angeles.
